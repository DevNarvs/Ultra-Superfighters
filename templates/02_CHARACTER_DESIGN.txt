ROW 0 — IDLE (4 frames, looping)
---------------------------------
F0: Neutral standing pose, relaxed, weight centered.
    Base pose — all other animations reference this.
F1: Slight inhale — body rises 1-2px, chest expands subtly.
    Keep feet at y=58.
F2: Peak of breath — [element-specific idle trait].
    Fire=hand glow. Lightning=small sparks. Wind=hair floats.
F3: Exhale — body lowers back to F0.
    Smooth return for seamless loop.

ROW 1 — RUN (6 frames, looping)
---------------------------------
F0: Right foot contact, left arm forward, right arm back.
F1: Right foot down, body lowers (lowest point).
F2: Right foot passing, body centered, legs cross.
F3: Left foot contact, right arm forward (mirror of F0).
F4: Left foot down, body lowers (mirror of F1).
F5: Left foot passing, body rises (mirror of F2, loops to F0).
