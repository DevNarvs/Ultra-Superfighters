COMBAT IDENTITY
================================================================================

Playstyle Summary
-----------------
Relentless Berserker who chains flowing, dance-like katana slashes with solar
fire enhancement. Every attack flows into the next like a ritual dance — each
"form" of the Kagura builds momentum. Hiro gets STRONGER the longer a combo
or exchange goes on, rewarding aggressive, unrelenting pressure. His attacks
leave lingering fire arcs that briefly deny space. High damage, high speed,
but below-average HP — he must keep the pressure on or get punished. The
fight is a dance, and Hiro leads.

ABILITY DETAILS
================================================================================

Basic Melee: Solar Kagura (3-hit combo)
---------------------------------------
The combo flows like a traditional dance — each slash transitions smoothly
into the next with no wasted movement. Fire trails follow the blade.

Hit 1             : 10 dmg — First Form: Dawn Strike. A quick horizontal
                    slash from left to right at chest height. The blade
                    sweeps cleanly with a brief orange fire trail. Fast
                    startup, low commitment. The opening step of the dance.
Hit 2             : 16 dmg — Second Form: Rising Sun. An upward diagonal
                    slash from low-right to upper-left. Hiro's body rises
                    with the cut, feet lifting slightly. Wider arc than
                    Hit 1 with stronger knockback. The ascending step.
Hit 3             : 24 dmg — Third Form: Setting Blaze. A powerful spinning
                    overhead downward slash. Hiro pivots with the blade
                    coming down in a fiery arc. Highest damage, widest fire
                    trail. The finishing step — the dance complete.
Combo Window      : 400ms (tight — rewards commitment and rhythm)
Cooldown          : 500ms between combos
Notes             : Each hit in the combo has a brief fire arc trail added
                    by the engine. The combo flows like a choreographed
                    dance — each pose transitions into the next with fluid
                    body rotation. Damage is HIGH tier (Berserker class).


Primary Power: Blazing Dawn Slash
---------------------------------
Damage            : 18
Speed             : Instant (dash attack, not projectile)
Range             : ~80px forward dash
Cooldown          : 8s
Knockback         : Medium / 300
Special           : Hiro dashes forward with a powerful lunging slash. During
                    the dash, a fire arc trails behind the blade. The slash
                    covers ~80px of horizontal distance very quickly. Enemies
                    hit are launched upward slightly (-150 kbY). After the
                    slash, Hiro ends in a low finishing pose ~80px ahead of
                    where he started.
                    This is a GAP CLOSER — it gets Hiro into melee range
                    instantly. High commitment (can dash off platforms or
                    into danger). If it whiffs, Hiro is stuck in the ending
                    pose briefly (punish window).
Visual            : Hiro lunges forward trailing a wide golden-orange fire
                    arc. The arc lingers for ~0.3s after the dash. Impact
                    creates a burst of solar sparks.


Secondary Ability: Sun's Ember
------------------------------
Duration          : 4s
Cooldown          : 14s
Special           : Hiro sheathes his katana briefly (iaido stance), then
                    draws it with a flash. For 4 seconds, all melee attacks
                    deal +5 bonus damage and leave larger fire trails. The
                    sun mark on his forehead blazes at maximum brightness.
                    A faint solar aura surrounds him during the buff.
                    Does NOT stack — reactivating refreshes the duration.
                    This is a DAMAGE WINDOW — Hiro pops this before going
                    all-in for maximum combo damage.
Visual            : Brief iaido draw flash, then golden-orange aura around
                    the character for the duration. Sun mark glow intensifies.
                    Fire trails on attacks become brighter and wider.


Dodge: Ember Step (Universal)
-----------------------------
Duration          : 1s invulnerability
Cooldown          : 15s
Visual            : Hiro's body bursts into a cloud of floating embers and
                    sparks. His silhouette briefly dissolves into scattered
                    orange-gold particles before reforming. During the dodge,
                    a faint afterimage of embers remains where he was.


Ultimate: Hinokami — Burning Sun
--------------------------------
Charge Source     : Dealing damage (1% per 5 dmg — fast, rewards aggression),
                    taking damage (1% per 10 dmg), passive (0.4%/s)
Startup Time      : 0.6s (fast startup — he's a Berserker, not a slow caster)
Damage            : 35 (total across all slashes hitting same target)
Knockback         : High / 400 (final slash)
Duration          : ~1.5s of rapid slashing, then finishing pose
Counterplay       : Dodgeable during the startup. Once the slashing begins,
                    each individual slash can be dodged but they come very
                    fast. Can be blocked (if blocking is added later). The
                    short range means Hiro must be CLOSE to land it.
Notes             : Hiro enters a trance-like state — the Hinokami Kagura
                    dance at its peak. He performs a rapid sequence of
                    circular fire slashes around himself, each leaving burning
                    arcs. The final slash is a massive horizontal sweep that
                    launches the target. The entire sequence is enclosed in
                    a brief dome of intersecting fire arcs.
                    This is a CLOSE-RANGE ultimate — Hiro must be right next
                    to the target. It does NOT send a projectile across the
                    arena. It rewards the aggressive Berserker playstyle of
                    getting in and staying in. If no one is nearby, it whiffs
                    and the charge is wasted.
Visual            : Hiro's sun mark blazes blinding gold. Camera flash golden-
                    white. Rapid circular fire slashes create a dome of
                    intersecting arcs around him. The final slash leaves
                    a massive sun-shaped fire pattern that lingers for ~1s.
                    Most visually intense effect of any Berserker character.


CHARACTER BALANCE NOTES
================================================================================
Strengths         : Highest combo damage when all 3 hits land + Sun's Ember
                    buff active. Blazing Dawn Slash is an excellent gap closer.
                    Fast attack speed (Berserker class). Strong pressure game —
                    once Hiro is in, he's hard to shake off. Ultimate is
                    devastating at close range. Dance-like flow makes combos
                    feel natural and rhythmic.
Weaknesses        : Below-average HP (105 with Innate bonus). No ranged
                    attacks — must close distance. Blazing Dawn Slash is
                    punishable on whiff. Ultimate requires close range — can
                    whiff entirely if the opponent dodges at startup. No
                    defensive utility — Sun's Ember is offensive, not
                    protective. Weak to zoners who keep distance (Mortis,
                    mages). Must stay aggressive or loses neutral.
Matchup vs Rina   : Mirror-like aggression matchup. Both are fast and fragile.
                    Hiro has higher HP (105 vs 85) and more damage per hit.
                    Rina has higher speed (230 vs 210) and better evasion
                    (blink). Comes down to who lands their combo first. Hiro
                    wins extended exchanges, Rina wins hit-and-run.
Matchup vs Mortis : Hiro wants to get inside Mortis's scythe range with
                    Blazing Dawn Slash. If he gets in, Mortis can't handle
                    the pressure — Hiro's fast attacks overwhelm Mortis's
                    slow recovery. But getting in is the problem — Wraith
                    Bolt and Soul Drain keep Hiro at bay. Mortis punishes
                    any whiffed Blazing Dawn Slash with Grave Cleave.
