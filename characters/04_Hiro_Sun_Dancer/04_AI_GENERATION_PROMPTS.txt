HOW TO USE
================================================================================
1. Copy ONE prompt at a time into your AI image generator.
2. Generate, review, adjust if needed.
3. After generating all 11 animation strips (A0-A10), stitch vertically:
   - Row 0 at y=0, Row 1 at y=64, Row 2 at y=128, ... Row 10 at y=640
   - Final sheet: 384 x 704 pixels (or 768 x 1408 at 2x scale)
4. Skill effects (B1-B6) are separate individual files.
5. Use Photopea.com (free) or Photoshop. "Nearest Neighbor" resampling.

WARM PALETTE WARNING — READ THIS CAREFULLY:
Hiro is the WARMEST, BRIGHTEST character in the entire roster — the visual
opposite of Mortis, Rina, and Karasu. Common AI generator mistakes for Hiro:
  1. OUTLINE too dark — Hiro uses warm dark brown (#1A0F0A), NOT pure black.
     If the generator produces black outlines, recolor to #1A0F0A in post.
  2. FIRE colors wrong — Hiro's energy is GOLDEN-ORANGE solar fire (#FFD700,
     #FF8C00, #FFF5CC), NOT red fire, NOT blue, NOT green. If fire effects
     look like standard red-orange campfire, shift toward gold.
  3. KIMONO too bright — The navy kimono (#1A1A3E) provides CONTRAST against
     the warm accents. Do NOT brighten it. The navy stays dark; the gold
     trim (#FFD700) pops against it.
  4. SUN MARK invisible — AI generators often lose the forehead sun mark at
     small pixel sizes. If it's missing, hand-paint it: 1 bright gold pixel
     center with 4-6 radiating 1px lines. It MUST be visible in every frame.
  5. KATANA missing or wrong — The sun-shaped tsuba (guard) is golden (#FFD700)
     and circular. The blade is steel (#C0C8D0), NOT golden. AI generators
     sometimes make the whole weapon gold — only the tsuba and trim are gold.
  6. HAKAMA too tight — The hakama are WIDE-LEGGED traditional pants that
     billow and flow. They should create a flowing lower-body silhouette,
     not skinny jeans.
Overall, the character should feel like SUNLIGHT IN HUMAN FORM — warm, bright,
heroic, determined. Increase warmth/color temperature in post if needed.
