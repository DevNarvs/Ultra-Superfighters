Appearance
----------
Hair              : Long straight jet black (#0A0A14) hair, tied in a low loose
                    ponytail that hangs behind the back. Side-parted bangs frame
                    the face — long enough to partially shadow one eye in some
                    frames. Hair has a subtle dark violet (#2A1A3E) sheen in the
                    highlights (not pure black — slightly purple-tinted).
Eyes              : Crimson red (#CC0000) glowing eyes — the KEY IDENTIFYING
                    FEATURE. The irises glow with a steady red light. In side
                    view, one eye is fully visible, the second partially visible.
                    During abilities, tiny spinning marks appear around the iris
                    (3 small dots in a circular pattern — 1-2px each of bright
                    crimson #FF0000). The eyes are calm, cold, and piercing —
                    no anger, no emotion, just absolute focus.
                    Eye glow INTENSIFIES during attacks and ultimate.
                    Eye glow DIMS when taking damage or dying.
Build             : Lean, tall, narrow frame. Slightly taller than average
                    (~52-54px). Elegant and precise, not bulky. Moves with
                    minimal wasted motion.
Outfit            : Dark high-collared cloak (#0F0F1A near-black) over a fitted
                    dark crimson (#4A0000) tunic. The cloak is long, reaching
                    to mid-calf, with an open front showing the tunic beneath.
                    The cloak has a high collar that frames the jaw and lower
                    face. Dark charcoal (#2A2A2A) fitted pants. The cloak
                    billows and flows during movement — it has volume and weight.
                    Thin dark red (#660000) trim lines along the cloak's edges.
Hands             : Pale skin (#F0DCC8) hands with dark fingerless gloves
                    (#1A1018). Fingers are long, precise — a spellcaster's hands.
Boots             : Dark (#1A1018) mid-calf boots, simple and functional. Thin
                    dark red (#660000) accent line at the top edge.
Extras            : SPECTRAL BLADE — NOT a physical weapon. Karasu fights by
                    materializing a ghostly violet (#6B3FA0) energy blade from
                    his cursed eye power. The blade appears during attack frames
                    and dissolves after. During idle, NO weapon is visible — he
                    stands with empty hands, calm and unarmed. The blade is
                    straight, katana-length (~36px), semi-transparent with a
                    bright violet (#9B59B6) edge glow and dark core (#2A1A3E).
                    CROWS — 1-2 small black crows perch on or near the character
                    during idle. They scatter during attacks. Each crow is tiny
                    (~4-5px body, dark shape with a small red #CC0000 eye dot).
                    Crows are a recurring motif — they appear in dodge, death,
                    and several ability effects.

COMBAT IDENTITY
================================================================================

Playstyle Summary
-----------------
Cold, calculated Slayer who controls the fight at mid-range with a spectral
blade and dark flame projectiles. Karasu is patient — he waits for the
opponent to commit, then punishes with devastating single strikes. His
spectral blade has excellent range (it materializes at distance), and his
Black Flame projectile deals damage over time, adding pressure even when he
doesn't land direct hits. The Crow Substitution decoy punishes aggressive
opponents who rush in. When his ultimate is ready, the Susanoo Awakening
transforms him into a spectral warrior for one overwhelming strike. Every
ability rewards patience and reads. He doesn't chase — he waits and strikes
with certainty.

ABILITY DETAILS
================================================================================

Basic Melee: Phantom Edge (3-hit combo)
---------------------------------------
The spectral blade materializes from nothing for each strike — it appears
at the moment of the slash and dissolves after. Each attack is a precise,
calculated cut with minimal wasted movement.

Hit 1             : 7 dmg — Shadow Cut: a clean horizontal spectral blade
                    swipe. The blade materializes mid-swing at full extension.
                    Long reach (~38px) but slow startup. The character barely
                    moves — only the arm extends as the blade appears. Cold,
                    efficient, surgical.
Hit 2             : 14 dmg — Phantom Sweep: a wider diagonal downward slash.
                    The blade is larger and brighter in this strike. Body
                    steps forward slightly. Greater knockback. The sweep
                    covers a wide arc.
Hit 3             : 22 dmg — Crimson Severance: the most devastating basic
                    strike. The eyes blaze maximum crimson as a massive
                    spectral blade materializes — larger than the normal
                    blade (~44px reach). A full-commitment overhead chop.
                    Extremely slow recovery. If it hits, devastating. If it
                    misses, punishable.
Combo Window      : 500ms (slow, deliberate — like Mortis)
Cooldown          : 600ms between combos
Notes             : The spectral blade appearing and disappearing is the
                    unique visual identity of this combo. Other characters
                    hold physical weapons — Karasu's weapon MANIFESTS from
                    his eye power for each individual strike.


Primary Power: Black Flame
--------------------------
Damage            : 8 initial + 4 per tick x 3 ticks = 20 total
Speed             : 280px/s (SLOW — menacing, not fast)
Range             : Full arena width
Cooldown          : 10s
Knockback         : Low / 150 (the flame sticks, it doesn't launch)
Special           : A dark flame projectile that moves slowly across the
                    arena. On contact, it deals 8 initial damage and
                    IGNITES the target — dealing 4 damage per second for
                    3 seconds (12 DOT). The total damage (20) is high but
                    spread over time. The slow speed makes it easy to jump
                    over but hard to ignore — it forces movement. Cannot be
                    cancelled by other projectiles (passes through them like
                    Wraith Bolt). Destroyed on contact with a player.
Visual            : A small sphere of BLACK fire — dark core (#050505) with
                    a dim red (#880000) outer glow. The flames flicker with
                    dark, unnatural movement. When it hits, dark flames
                    briefly engulf the target (engine handles the DOT visual).


Secondary Ability: Crow Substitution
-------------------------------------
Duration          : Decoy lasts 3s or until hit
Cooldown          : 12s
Damage            : 10 (explosion on contact)
Special           : Karasu leaves a CROW CLONE at his current position and
                    instantly shifts backward ~40px. The clone looks like a
                    dark silhouette of Karasu made of clustered crows — a
                    vaguely human shape composed of black birds. If an enemy
                    attacks the clone, it EXPLODES into a burst of crows and
                    dark energy, dealing 10 damage and briefly stunning.
                    If not hit within 3 seconds, the clone dissolves into
                    crows that fly away harmlessly.
                    This is a COUNTER/TRAP — it punishes aggressive opponents
                    who attack without thinking. Karasu bait → clone explodes
                    → free punish with Phantom Edge.
Visual            : Clone is a dark human silhouette made of clustered crow
                    shapes. Red eye dots visible on the clone. On explosion:
                    burst of crows scattering in all directions + dark
                    energy flash.


Dodge: Crow Scatter (Universal)
-------------------------------
Duration          : 1s invulnerability
Cooldown          : 15s
Visual            : Karasu's body BURSTS into a murder of crows. The solid
                    form shatters into 8-12 individual small black crow
                    silhouettes that scatter in all directions — some fly
                    upward, some sideways, some in loops. During the 1s
                    dodge, the scattered crows swirl and circle in the air
                    where the character was. Then they converge back together,
                    reforming into Karasu's solid body. Each crow is a tiny
                    dark shape (~4-5px) with a single red eye dot.
                    The most visually distinctive dodge in the game — the
                    character literally becomes a flock of birds.


Ultimate: Susanoo Awakening
----------------------------
Charge Source     : Dealing damage (1% per 7 dmg — slower, rewards patience),
                    taking damage (1% per 8 dmg — faster on defense),
                    passive (0.3%/s — slowest passive, must fight to charge)
Startup Time      : 0.8s (slow, dramatic)
Damage            : 45 (single devastating slash — highest single-hit in game)
Knockback         : Massive / 500
Duration          : ~2s total (0.8s startup + ~1.2s slash + aftermath)
Counterplay       : Long, visible startup — the Susanoo forming around Karasu
                    is a clear warning. Jumpable (the slash is a wide horizontal
                    sweep). Dodgeable. But if it hits, it's the hardest single
                    hit in the entire game (45 damage).
Notes             : Karasu's eyes blaze maximum crimson. A massive spectral
                    warrior — the Susanoo — forms around and above him. The
                    Susanoo is a translucent violet-red spectral armored giant
                    (~2x character height) that engulfs Karasu inside it. The
                    Susanoo raises an enormous spectral fire blade overhead,
                    then delivers one devastating downward slash that covers
                    a massive area in front. The slash leaves a wave of dark
                    fire on the ground that lingers briefly.
                    This is the SINGLE BIGGEST HIT in the game — one strike,
                    maximum damage. If it connects, the round is likely over.
                    If it misses, the long recovery leaves Karasu completely
                    vulnerable.
Visual            : The Susanoo is a translucent spectral warrior outline —
                    ghostly violet (#7B4FBF) body with bright lavender
                    (#B088D0) edges and deep purple (#3D1F6D) shadows. Hellfire
                    red (#FF2200) energy courses through it. The spectral
                    blade it wields is enormous — wider than the character frame.
                    Screen flash red-white on activation. Camera shake on impact.


CHARACTER BALANCE NOTES
================================================================================
Strengths         : Highest single-hit damage in the game (Susanoo — 45 dmg).
                    Black Flame DOT adds passive pressure. Crow Substitution
                    punishes reckless aggression. Spectral blade range is
                    excellent. Patient playstyle is oppressive once mastered.
                    Every ability has a purpose in the spacing game.
Weaknesses        : Slowest attack speed (120ms frames). Low mobility (180 spd).
                    No gap closer — must wait for opponents to come to him.
                    Extremely punishable on whiffed attacks. Ultimate is the
                    most committal in the game (45 dmg but huge punish window
                    on miss). Weak to fast rushdown (Rina, Hiro) who can
                    bait and punish. Black Flame is easy to jump over.
Matchup vs Rina   : Karasu struggles — Rina's speed overwhelms his slow attacks.
                    Crow Substitution is his best tool (bait Rina's aggression
                    → clone explodes → punish). If Rina respects the clone,
                    Karasu can zone with Black Flame. But if Rina gets inside,
                    Karasu can't keep up.
Matchup vs Mortis : Battle of patience. Both are slow Slayers who wait for
                    openings. Karasu's Black Flame passes through Wraith Bolt
                    (both are intangible projectiles). Mortis has more HP but
                    Karasu has higher burst. Comes down to who lands their
                    ultimate first. Mortis's Death's Harvest vs Karasu's
                    Susanoo — a clash of ultimate abilities.
Matchup vs Hiro   : Karasu's worst matchup. Hiro's Blazing Dawn Slash closes
                    distance instantly, and his fast attack speed overwhelms
                    Karasu's slow recovery. Karasu must rely entirely on Crow
                    Substitution reads and well-placed Black Flames to keep
                    Hiro at bay. If Susanoo lands, Hiro dies. If it misses,
                    Hiro kills Karasu during the recovery.
