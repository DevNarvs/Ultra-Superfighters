HOW TO USE
================================================================================
1. Copy ONE prompt at a time into your AI image generator.
2. Generate, review, adjust if needed.
3. After generating all 11 animation strips (A0-A10), stitch vertically:
   - Row 0 at y=0, Row 1 at y=64, Row 2 at y=128, ... Row 10 at y=640
   - Final sheet: 384 x 704 pixels (or 768 x 1408 at 2x scale)
4. Skill effects (B1-B6) are separate individual files.
5. Use Photopea.com (free) or Photoshop. "Nearest Neighbor" resampling.

VERY DARK PALETTE WARNING:
Karasu is the DARKEST character in the game — nearly all black with crimson
accents. AI generators will try to brighten the cloak. You WILL need to darken
results in post-processing. The cloak should be almost indistinguishable from
true black (#0F0F1A). The ONLY bright elements are the crimson eyes (#CC0000),
spectral blade glow (#9B59B6), and pale skin (#F0DCC8). Everything else is
near-black. Crow details are tiny — don't expect perfect crows at this scale.
