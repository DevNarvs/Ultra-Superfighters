CHARACTER IDENTITY
================================================================================
Name              : Mortis
Title / Alias     : The Soul Reaper
Element / Theme   : Shadow / Death (manifests as cyan-green spectral energy)
Role              : Zoner (scythe range, spectral projectiles, ground traps)
Personality       : Silent, ominous, inevitable


VISUAL IDENTITY
================================================================================

Appearance
----------
Hair              : None visible — head fully concealed under deep pointed hood.
                    Only shadow and glowing eyes visible beneath.
Eyes              : Two floating cyan-green glowing dots under the hood — eerie,
                    piercing, no pupils. The ONLY facial feature. Eyes pulse
                    brighter during attacks and dim when hit.
Build             : Tall and gaunt, skeletal silhouette under flowing robes.
                    Semi-chibi proportions but uses more vertical frame space
                    (52-56px) than other fighters — imposing presence.
Outfit            : Long tattered dark robe, torn edges that fray into wispy
                    tendrils at the bottom hem. High collar that blends into
                    the hood. Robe is layered — outer layer is darkest, inner
                    layer slightly lighter where visible at the tears and openings.
Belt / Accessory  : Thin chain belt at the waist with a small ghostly green
                    gem/pendant hanging from the center. Bony angular shoulder
                    pauldrons (dark iron) with faint green rune etchings.
Hands             : Skeletal bone-white hands visible emerging from robe sleeves.
                    Bony fingers, slightly elongated. No flesh — just pale bone.
Feet / Boots      : NO visible feet. Robe extends all the way to ground line,
                    tattered bottom edges touch y=58. Character appears to float
                    or glide rather than walk. Slight 1-2px gap under robe in
                    idle suggests hovering.
Extras            : SCYTHE — the defining feature. Dark iron shaft, curved silver
                    blade with faint cyan-green edge glow. Carried in the right
                    hand. Visible in nearly every frame. The scythe IS this
                    character's silhouette — must be recognizable at a glance.
                    Robe tendrils sway and trail during movement (the robes
                    behave like they have their own slow, ghostly physics).

COMBAT IDENTITY
================================================================================

Playstyle Summary
-----------------
Patient, oppressive zoner who controls space with long-range scythe swings
and spectral projectiles. Places cursed ground traps to deny area and punishes
aggressive opponents who rush in recklessly. Lower health and slower movement
than average — compensated by superior range on ALL melee attacks (scythe)
and strong keep-away tools. Rewards patient, defensive play and spacing mastery.
Glass cannon with reach.


Abilities Overview
------------------
Slot                  Name                    Type              Short Description
----                  ----                    ----              -----------------
Basic Melee           Reaper's Harvest        Combo (3-hit)     Scythe slashes — slow but long range
Primary Power         Wraith Bolt             Projectile        Spectral skull, horizontal, passes through projectiles
Secondary Ability     Soul Drain              Ground Trap       Cursed circle, DOT + slow to enemies standing in it
Dodge                 Shadow Step (Universal) Invulnerability   Dissolves into shadow — 1s invuln, 15s cooldown
Ultimate              Death's Harvest         Wave/Area         Massive spectral scythe wave + lingering soul wisps


ABILITY DETAILS
================================================================================

Basic Melee: Reaper's Harvest
-----------------------------
Hit 1             : 7 dmg — Crypt Slash: quick horizontal scythe swipe at
                    chest height. Shortest range of the three but still
                    longer than most characters' melee (scythe advantage).
Hit 2             : 14 dmg — Reaping Arc: wide overhead scythe swing, large
                    sweeping downward arc. Good range (~40px reach), strong
                    knockback.
Hit 3             : 22 dmg — Grave Cleave: full-body overhead scythe slam
                    into the ground. Massive arc (~44px reach), highest
                    damage, but VERY slow recovery (punishable on whiff).
Combo Window      : 500ms (wider window than most — slow but deliberate)
Cooldown          : 600ms between combos
Notes             : Slowest melee combo in the game but highest range and
                    damage per hit. Each swing covers massive space. Hit 3
                    is extremely committal — miss it and you're vulnerable
                    for almost half a second.

Primary Power: Wraith Bolt
--------------------------
Damage            : 12
Speed             : 400px/s (slower than fireball — more menacing)
Range             : Full arena width
Cooldown          : 6s
Knockback         : Moderate / 250
Special           : Spectral skull projectile. PASSES THROUGH enemy projectiles
                    (cannot be cancelled by other projectiles). Can only be
                    avoided by jumping or dodging. Destroyed on contact with
                    a player. Slightly larger hitbox than fireball (28x28)
                    making it harder to jump over.
Visual            : Ghostly cyan-green floating skull, hollow eye sockets,
                    mouth open in silent scream. Wispy vapor trail behind.

Secondary Ability: Soul Drain
-----------------------------
Duration          : 3s (circle persists on the ground)
Damage            : 5 dmg per tick, 3 ticks over 3 seconds (15 total if
                    opponent stands in it the entire time)
Cooldown          : 14s
Range             : Placed at Mortis's current position on the ground
Special           : Creates a dark runic circle (~80px diameter) on the
                    ground. Enemies standing in the circle take 5 damage
                    every second AND are slowed by 30% movement speed.
                    Mortis himself is NOT affected by his own circle.
                    Circle disappears after 3 seconds.
                    Excellent for area denial — force opponents to move
                    where you want them, then punish with scythe.

Ultimate: Death's Harvest
-------------------------
Charge Source     : Dealing damage (1% per 6 dmg), taking damage (1% per
                    12 dmg), passive (0.5%/s — slower passive, rewards combat)
Startup Time      : 0.8s (slowest startup — dramatic and cinematic)
Damage            : 40 direct hit from the wave
Knockback         : Massive / 500
Duration          : Wave travels across arena in ~2s. Leaves 3 soul wisps
                    on the ground that persist for 4 seconds.
Counterplay       : Jumpable (wave is ground-level, ~50px tall). Dodgeable
                    with Phase Shift. Long startup is visible — glowing eyes,
                    rising energy, dramatic pose. Punishable if read correctly.
Aftermath         : Leaves 3 soul wisps spread across the arena floor.
                    Each wisp deals 3 dmg on contact (one-time, not DOT).
                    Wisps float slightly and drift slowly. Last 4 seconds.
Notes             : Mortis raises scythe overhead, dark energy swirls inward,
                    then sweeps a massive spectral scythe-blade wave across
                    the entire arena floor. Most dramatic ultimate in the game.
                    The wave is shaped like an enormous curved scythe blade
                    made of ghostly energy.


CHARACTER BALANCE NOTES
================================================================================
Strengths         : Longest melee range in the game (scythe), projectile
                    that passes through other projectiles, area denial with
                    Soul Drain, massive damage on committed hits, oppressive
                    zoning game, intimidating presence
Weaknesses        : Lowest move speed (180 vs standard 200), below-average
                    health (90 vs standard 100), slowest attack recovery,
                    very punishable if attacks whiff, weak to rushdown
                    characters who get inside scythe range, floaty jump
                    (lower jump force)
Matchup vs Fire   : Mortis wants to keep distance and zone with Wraith Bolt
                    (which passes through fireballs). Fire Battlemancer wants
                    to bait out scythe swings and punish the long recovery.
                    Flame Shield blocks Wraith Bolt. Soul Drain forces Fire
                    to move out of fireball zoning positions.
Matchup vs Rina   : Mortis struggles against Rina's speed. Shadow Blink lets
                    Rina teleport inside scythe range. Mortis relies on Soul
                    Drain placement and reading Rina's blink timing. If Rina
                    gets close, Mortis is in serious trouble. If Mortis keeps
                    distance, the scythe range advantage is dominant.


AI PROMPT: CHARACTER CONCEPT
================================================================================

Pixel art character design sheet for a 2D side-scrolling fighting game.

CHARACTER: "Mortis, the Soul Reaper" — a Grim Reaper inspired shadow/death
battlemancer with spectral cyan-green energy.

CAMERA / VIEW:
- Strict side view (profile), facing RIGHT.
- The character is viewed from a 90-degree side angle, like a classic 2D
  fighting game (Street Fighter, King of Fighters pixel art style).
- NOT 3/4 view. NOT front-facing. NOT isometric. Pure flat side profile.

PROPORTIONS & SIZE:
- Semi-chibi anime proportions: head is approximately 1/3 of total body height.
- Total character height: approximately 52-56 pixels tall (taller than average).
- Character width: approximately 24-32 pixels wide at widest point.
- The character must fit inside a 64x64 pixel cell with padding above the
  head and below the feet.
- Robe bottom touches ground line at y=58 (6 pixels from bottom of 64px frame).

APPEARANCE (DETAILED):
- HOOD: Deep pointed hood covering the entire head. The hood is part of the
  robe — seamless transition. Interior of the hood is pure black (#050510),
  creating a void-like shadow where the face should be. The hood's point
  extends slightly upward. In side view, the hood profile shows the pointed
  tip and the deep shadow underneath.
- EYES: Two small glowing cyan-green (#00FFAA) dots floating in the darkness
  under the hood. These are the ONLY facial feature. No nose, no mouth, no
  skin — just two eerie floating eye-dots in blackness. They are the KEY
  identifying feature and must be clearly visible in every frame. In side
  view, one eye is fully visible, the second partially visible behind it.
- BODY: Gaunt, tall, skeletal silhouette. The body shape is mostly defined by
  the robe draping over a thin frame. Angular shoulders visible through the
  fabric. No visible flesh except the hands.
- ROBE: Long flowing robe in deep charcoal (#1A1A2E) with near-black blue
  (#0F0F1A) in the deepest folds. The robe extends from the hood all the way
  to the ground. The bottom hem is TORN and TATTERED — fraying into wispy
  tendrils and ragged strips. The robe has some volume and layers — outer
  layer is darkest, inner visible at tears. Highlight edges in dark slate
  (#2D2D44). In side view, the robe creates a tall, narrow, flowing silhouette.
- SHOULDER PAULDRONS: Small angular dark iron (#333344) shoulder guards on
  both shoulders. They have faint green (#228866) runic etch marks — just
  1-2 pixels of dim green to suggest magical runes. Bony and angular shape.
- CHAIN BELT: Thin chain belt (#444455) at the waist with a small glowing
  cyan-green (#00FFAA) gem pendant hanging from the center front.
- HANDS: Skeletal bone-white (#D4C8A8) hands with darker shadows (#A89878).
  Bony fingers, slightly elongated and claw-like. Clearly skeletal — no flesh,
  just pale bone. Visible emerging from the wide robe sleeves.
- SCYTHE: Held in the right hand. Dark iron (#333344) shaft, approximately
  40-45px long (nearly the character's height). Curved blade at the top
  with silver metal (#AABBCC) and a faint cyan-green (#00CC88) glow along
  the cutting edge. The scythe is this character's DEFINING feature — its
  silhouette should be instantly recognizable. In side view, the shaft is
  mostly vertical with the blade curving forward (to the right).
- FEET: NOT VISIBLE. The tattered robe extends to the ground line completely
  concealing any feet. The ragged bottom edge of the robe touches y=58.
  There should be a subtle 1-2px gap visible under parts of the tattered hem
  suggesting the character hovers slightly off the ground.

PIXEL ART STYLE:
- Clean 1-pixel dark outline (#0A0A14) around the entire character silhouette.

- No anti-aliasing — hard pixel edges only.

- Anime-style pixel art, similar to "Warrior Girls Pack" or "Street Fighter

- Dark, gothic, ethereal mood — this character should feel ominous and
  otherworldly compared to the brighter fighters.

OUTPUT:
