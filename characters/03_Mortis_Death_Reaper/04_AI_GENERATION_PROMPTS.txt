  INPUT:    <- 01_CHARACTER_DESCRIPTION.txt
            <- 02_CHARACTER_DESIGN.txt
            <- 03_CHARACTER_SKILLS_EFFECTS.txt
  OUTPUT:   Individual sprite strips → stitch into full sprite sheet + effects

HOW TO USE
================================================================================
1. Copy ONE prompt at a time into your AI image generator.
2. Generate, review, adjust if needed.
3. After generating all 11 animation strips (A0-A10), stitch vertically:
   - Row 0 at y=0, Row 1 at y=64, Row 2 at y=128, ... Row 10 at y=640
   - Final sheet: 384 x 704 pixels (or 768 x 1408 at 2x scale)
4. Skill effects (B1-B6) are separate individual files.
5. Use Photopea.com (free) or Photoshop. "Nearest Neighbor" resampling.

DARK PALETTE WARNING:
AI generators tend to make things brighter than requested. You may need to
darken the results in post-processing. The void areas should be VERY dark
(#050510, #0F0F1A) — almost black. The only bright elements are the cyan-green
edges, eye glow, and scythe blade glow.

fire spark. The void-dark center is key: scythe cuts don't create light,
