CHARACTER IDENTITY
================================================================================
Name              : Rina
Title / Alias     : The Phantom Blade
Element / Theme   : Shadow / Void (manifests as pink energy)
Role              : Rush-down / Assassin
Personality       : Calm, calculated, deadly


VISUAL IDENTITY
================================================================================

Appearance
----------
Hair              : Long straight hair tied in a low ponytail, pastel pink with
                    darker magenta tips. Side-swept bangs framing face. Hair flows
                    slightly behind during movement.
Eyes              : Sharp violet-pink eyes behind stylish rectangular glasses.
                    Confident, analytical expression. Glasses reflect light (small
                    white pixel glint on lens).
Build             : Slim and agile, semi-chibi proportions. Slightly shorter/leaner
                    than Fire Battlemancer — emphasizes speed over power.
Outfit            : Fitted dark magenta assassin coat, cropped at the waist, with

                    Coat has subtle diamond pattern trim on the edges.
Belt / Accessory  : Black utility belt with small pink gem buckle. Two small
                    pouches on the back hip (kunai holsters).
Hands             : Black fingerless gloves. Hands glow with faint pink-violet
                    shadow energy when casting abilities.
Feet / Boots      : Sleek black thigh-high boots with pink accent stripes on the
                    sides. Flat soles (assassin, not heels — needs to be fast).
Extras            : Rectangular glasses (key visual trait — always visible).
                    Faint pink shadow aura trails behind when moving fast.
                    Small scarf/ribbon tied at the collar, flutters during movement.

COMBAT IDENTITY
================================================================================

Playstyle Summary
-----------------
Lightning-fast close-range assassin who darts in with shadow blinks, shreds
opponents with rapid dagger combos, and vanishes before they can retaliate.
Low health pool but highest damage-per-second when in melee range. High skill
ceiling — rewards precise timing and aggressive positioning. Glass cannon.


Abilities Overview
------------------
Slot                  Name                    Type              Short Description
----                  ----                    ----              -----------------
Basic Melee           Shadow Fang Combo       Combo (3-hit)     Rapid dagger slashes — fast, precise, low recovery
Primary Power         Void Kunai              Projectile        Pink energy kunai, fast travel, pierces on kill
Secondary Ability     Shadow Blink            Teleport/Dash     Short-range teleport, leaves damaging afterimage
Dodge                 Phase Shift (Universal) Invulnerability   1s invulnerability, 15s cooldown
Ultimate              Phantom Barrage         Multi-hit Area    Teleports rapidly across arena slashing all enemies


ABILITY DETAILS
================================================================================

Basic Melee: Shadow Fang Combo
------------------------------
Hit 1             : 6 dmg — quick horizontal dagger slash, very fast, short range
Hit 2             : 6 dmg — reverse backhand slash, slightly wider arc
Hit 3             : 10 dmg — spinning downward slash, launches opponent slightly
Combo Window      : 350ms (tighter than Fire Battlemancer — rewards precision)
Cooldown          : 300ms between combos
Notes             : Fastest melee combo in the game. Low damage per hit but the
                    speed makes DPS competitive. Hit 3 has a small popup for
                    follow-up opportunities.

Primary Power: Void Kunai
-------------------------
Damage            : 12
Speed             : 600px/s (faster than fireball)
Range             : Full arena width
Cooldown          : 1.2s
Knockback         : Light / 200 (doesn't push far — keeps enemies in range)
Special           : Travels in a straight line. If it kills an enemy, it pierces
                    through and continues (won't happen in 1v1 but matters in FFA).
                    Smaller hitbox than fireball — harder to hit but faster.

Secondary Ability: Shadow Blink
-------------------------------
Duration          : Instant teleport (0.15s animation)
Damage            : 8 dmg from afterimage (at departure point, 0.5s duration)
Cooldown          : 6s
Range             : Teleports ~150px in the direction character is facing
Special           : Leaves a pink shadow afterimage at the starting position that
                    damages enemies who touch it for 0.5 seconds. Can be used in
                    any direction (forward/backward). Can blink through enemies.
                    Cannot blink through solid platforms (stops at platform edge).
                    Can be used in the air.

Ultimate: Phantom Barrage
-------------------------
Charge Source     : Dealing damage (1% per 8 dmg — rewards aggression),
                    taking damage (1% per 15 dmg), passive (0.8%/s)
Startup Time      : 0.4s (faster than Fire — assassin theme)
Damage            : 5 hits x 8 dmg = 40 total (same total as Fire, but multi-hit)
Knockback         : Final hit only — Strong / 500
Duration          : 2 seconds of rapid teleport-slashes across arena
Counterplay       : Each slash has a small gap — can be dodged with Phase Shift
                    if timed between hits. Opponent can also jump to avoid ground
                    slashes. Startup is visible (pink energy gathering).
Aftermath         : Final slash leaves a large shadow explosion (purely visual,
                    no lingering hazard — assassins don't leave evidence).
Notes             : Rina vanishes and reappears rapidly across the arena,
                    slashing through the target location 5 times. She's invulnerable
                    during the teleport phases but each slash has a brief moment
                    where she materializes. Most cinematic when it hits — rapid
                    slash lines appear across the screen.


CHARACTER BALANCE NOTES
================================================================================
Strengths         : Fastest melee, fastest projectile, has a teleport, high burst
Weaknesses        : Lowest HP in the game (85 vs standard 100), no defensive
                    ability (shield/armor), Shadow Blink afterimage is easy to
                    avoid, ultimate can be partially dodged
Matchup vs Fire   : Rina wants to blink past fireballs and combo up close.
                    Fire Battlemancer wants to zone with fireballs and use Flame
                    Shield to punish Rina's aggression. Skill-based matchup.
