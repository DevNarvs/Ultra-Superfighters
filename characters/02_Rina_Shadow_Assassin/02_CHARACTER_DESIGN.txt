FRAME-BY-FRAME POSE GUIDE
================================================================================

ROW 0 — IDLE (4 frames, looping)
---------------------------------
F0: Relaxed assassin stance. Weight on back foot. Right hand at side, left hand
    near hip (resting near belt/kunai pouch). Ponytail hangs naturally behind.
    Glasses clearly visible on the face with white glint pixel. Scarf hangs still.
    Expression: calm, composed. Feet at y=58.

F1: Subtle inhale — body rises 1px. Ponytail shifts very slightly. Hand near hip
    moves 1px upward as if ready to draw. Glasses glint stays in same position.

F2: Peak of breath. Faint pink glow hint on the left hand near hip (shadow energy
    ready). Ponytail sways 1-2px to the right. Scarf tails shift slightly.

F3: Exhale — body lowers back to F0 position. Glow fades. Smooth return for
    seamless loop. Must align perfectly with F0.


ROW 1 — RUN (6 frames, looping)
---------------------------------
F0: Right foot strikes ground ahead, left arm swings forward with fist,
    right arm back. Body leans forward ~5 degrees (assassin sprint, aggressive).
    Ponytail streams behind. Scarf flutters back. Glasses visible.

F1: Right foot planted, body lowers (lowest point). Arms mid-swing.
    Ponytail follows momentum, slightly higher.

F2: Right foot pushes off, body rises. Legs cross underneath. Arms passing
    center. Ponytail catches up to body.

F3: Left foot strikes ground (mirror of F0). Right arm forward, left arm back.

F4: Left foot planted, body lowers (mirror of F1).

F5: Left foot pushes off, legs cross (mirror of F2). Loops back to F0.

IMPORTANT: Ponytail and scarf must trail BEHIND the direction of movement
in every frame. They lag by 1-2 frames from body position (natural physics).


ROW 2 — JUMP (3 frames, one-shot)
---------------------------------
F0: LAUNCH — legs compressed and bent, crouching low, arms pull downward.
    Body coils. Ponytail compressed down. Feet still near y=58.

F1: PEAK — body airborne, legs tucked up slightly, arms out at sides for
    balance. Ponytail floats upward (weightless). Scarf floats up. Coat tails
    lift slightly. Character centered vertically in the frame.

F2: FALL — legs extend downward toward ground, arms rise up, head tilts
    slightly down (looking where landing). Ponytail streams upward (trailing
    from fall). Scarf above.


ROW 3 — ATTACK 1: Dagger Slash (3 frames, one-shot, 70ms)
---------------------------------
F0: WINDUP — right hand sweeps back behind the body, holding an invisible
    energy dagger (the blade is added by the engine as a pink glow). Body
    coils slightly. Left arm guards in front. Quick, tight motion.

F1: STRIKE — right arm slashes horizontally forward in a fast arc. Arm
    fully extended with hand at ~45px from left edge of frame (reaching
    forward). Body rotates into the slash. The slash trail will be added by
    the engine — just show the arm extended with an open hand/claw pose.

F2: RECOVERY — arm pulling back toward body. Body returning to neutral.
    Very fast recovery (assassin). Left arm drops guard.

IMPORTANT: The energy dagger blade is NOT drawn on the sprite. Only draw
the character's body and arm positions. The pink slash effect is added by
the game engine at runtime.


ROW 4 — ATTACK 2: Backhand Slash (3 frames, one-shot, 70ms)
---------------------------------
F0: WINDUP — left arm crosses in front of body from right to left, winding
    up for a backhand. Body twists slightly away from the target. Right arm
    pulled back for balance.

F1: STRIKE — left arm whips outward in a backhand slash from left to right.
    Full extension. Body rotates into the hit. The reverse motion from Attack 1
    makes the combo feel like alternating slashes.

F2: RECOVERY — left arm returning. Body untwisting. Slightly faster recovery
    than Attack 1 to maintain combo flow.


ROW 5 — ATTACK 3: Spinning Downward Slash (4 frames, one-shot, 90ms)
---------------------------------
F0: WINDUP — body begins to pivot. Both arms pull up and back. Weight shifts
    to front foot. Ponytail begins swinging.

F1: SPIN — body rotating, mid-spin. Arms sweeping in an arc. Ponytail whips
    around. This is a full-body spinning motion. Side view shows the torso
    turned slightly (shoulders overlapping).

F2: IMPACT — both arms slash downward diagonally. Maximum reach forward and
    down. Body lunged forward. This is the heaviest hit — pose should look
    powerful and committed. Ponytail fully extended from spin momentum.

F3: RECOVERY — body stumbles forward slightly from the spin momentum.
    Arms lowering. Longer recovery than other attacks (high risk/reward).
    Ponytail settling.


ROW 6 — CAST: Void Kunai Throw (4 frames, one-shot, 100ms)
---------------------------------
F0: PREPARE — right hand reaches behind to the belt pouch (kunai holster).
    Body slightly turned. Left arm out front for balance.

F1: DRAW — right hand pulls back past the ear in a throwing position.
    Body coils. Weight shifts to back foot. The kunai is NOT drawn on the
    sprite — only the hand position as if holding something small.

F2: THROW — right arm whips forward in a throwing motion, releasing at
    full extension. Body lunges forward. Arm at maximum forward reach.
    Wrist flicks. The pink kunai projectile is spawned by the engine at
    this frame.

F3: FOLLOW-THROUGH — arm continues forward motion, then begins returning.
    Body settles. Slight forward lean from the throw momentum.

IMPORTANT: Do NOT draw the kunai in the sprite. The game engine spawns
the Void Kunai projectile as a separate sprite at the hand position on F2.


ROW 7 — TAKE HIT (2 frames, one-shot)
---------------------------------
F0: IMPACT — body jerked backward. Head snaps back, glasses shift slightly
    on the face (pushed up by the impact). Pain expression — eyes squeeze,
    mouth opens. Ponytail whips forward from the backward momentum.

F1: RECOIL — body leans further back. One foot (front) lifts off ground.
    Arms flung outward from the impact force. Glasses still shifted.
    Ponytail still swinging forward. More exaggerated stagger.


ROW 8 — DODGE / PHASE SHIFT (2 frames, holds F1)
---------------------------------
F0: ENTER — arms cross in front of chest in an X shape, body tenses,
    crouching slightly. Glasses glint brighter (1-2 extra white pixels).
    Scarf pulls tight. Concentrating expression.

F1: ACTIVE — body relaxes into a ghostly pose. Arms slightly spread to
    sides, palms open. Standing straight. Serene, ethereal expression.
    Ponytail floats slightly (as if weightless/phasing). Scarf floats.
    The engine makes this frame semi-transparent and adds a blue-pink tint
    at runtime. Draw the character normally — engine handles the ghost effect.
    This frame is HELD for 1 full second during the dodge.


ROW 9 — ULTIMATE: Phantom Barrage (4 frames, one-shot, 180ms)
---------------------------------
F0: CHARGE — crouching low, one knee nearly touching ground. Both arms
    pulled in tight, fists clenched. Head down, glasses glinting. Pink
    shadow energy begins (engine adds the aura). Intense, focused expression.

F1: RISE — pushing up from crouch to standing. Arms sweeping outward.
    Ponytail rising with the motion. Body emanating power (engine adds
    pink energy aura). Expression shifts to determined/fierce.

F2: VANISH POSE — body in a dynamic forward-leaning sprint pose, as if
    about to launch. One arm back holding an energy blade position, other
    arm forward. Ponytail streams behind. This is the last visible frame
    before Rina teleports — should look like frozen mid-dash. Most dynamic
    and aggressive pose in the entire sheet.

F3: REAPPEAR — landing pose after the barrage. One knee down, one arm
    touching the ground (superhero landing style). Head slightly bowed.
    Ponytail settling. Glasses visible. Cool, composed "it's already over"
    expression. The engine returns to idle after this frame.


ROW 10 — DEATH (3 frames, one-shot, 200ms)
---------------------------------
F0: STAGGER — stumbling forward, one hand clutches the stomach/chest.
    Glasses slipping down the nose. Pain expression. Ponytail swings.
    Knees buckling.

F1: FALLING — body tilting sideways to the right, legs giving out. Arms
    go limp. Glasses falling off or hanging at an angle. Ponytail trailing
    the fall direction.

F2: DOWN — lying on the ground (body rotated approximately 80 degrees
    clockwise). Head on the right, feet on the left. Glasses on the ground
    nearby or still barely on the face. Ponytail spread on the ground.
    Must fit within the 64x64 frame. Peaceful/unconscious expression.
