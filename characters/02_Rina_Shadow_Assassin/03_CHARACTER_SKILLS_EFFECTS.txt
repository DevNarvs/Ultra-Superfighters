EFFECT 1: VOID KUNAI (Projectile)                                   [MUST HAVE]
================================================================================

File              : void_kunai.png
Frame size        : 32 x 32 pixels
Frames            : 4
Total sheet size  : 128 x 32 pixels
Animation         : Looping, 80ms per frame
Direction         : Traveling RIGHT (engine flips for left)
Engine adds       : Pink particle trail behind, faint glow aura

Layout:
  [ F0 ][ F1 ][ F2 ][ F3 ]
  32x32  32x32  32x32  32x32

FRAME DESCRIPTIONS:
  F0: A pink energy kunai shape, ~20px long x 8px wide, pointed toward the
      right. The shape is a classic kunai/dagger: pointed narrow tip on the
      right, wider at the base on the left with a small ring/loop at the tail.
      Bright hot pink (#FF69B4) body with a white (#FFFFFF) edge highlight on
      the blade. Darker magenta (#8B1A6E) shadow on the underside. Faint pink
      glow around the edges (2-3 pixels of semi-transparent #FFB6D9).
      Centered vertically in the 32x32 frame. Positioned slightly left of
      center (tip at about x=26, tail at about x=6).

  F1: Slight flicker — the energy kunai pulses. The glow around the edges
      expands by 1-2 pixels. The white highlight shifts slightly. The overall
      brightness increases subtly.

  F2: Glow contracts back. Small void/shadow wisps appear at the tail end
      (1-2 pixels of dark #2A1028 trailing behind). The kunai body remains
      the same shape and position.

  F3: Returns toward F0 appearance for seamless loop. Wisps fade.

DESIGN NOTES:
  - The kunai is an ENERGY construct, not a physical metal weapon. It should
    look like it's made of solidified pink light/shadow energy.
  - Semi-transparent pink glow around the edges is important for the energy feel.
  - Keep the shape clean and recognizable even at small size.
  - Tip points RIGHT. Engine flips the whole sprite for leftward travel.

EFFECT 2: KUNAI IMPACT (Explosion)                                  [MUST HAVE]
================================================================================

File              : void_kunai_impact.png
Frame size        : 64 x 64 pixels
Frames            : 5
Total sheet size  : 320 x 64 pixels
Animation         : One-shot, 50ms per frame (very fast)
Engine adds       : Small pink spark particles flying outward

Layout:
  [  F0  ][  F1  ][  F2  ][  F3  ][  F4  ]
   64x64   64x64   64x64   64x64   64x64

FRAME DESCRIPTIONS:
  F0: FLASH — a small bright white-pink flash at the center of the frame,
      approximately 12-16 pixels diameter. Pure white (#FFFFFF) center with
      a hot pink (#FF69B4) edge. Sharp starburst shape with 4-6 short rays.
      This is the instant of impact.

  F1: EXPAND — the flash blooms outward to approximately 28-32 pixels.
      The center shifts from white to bright pastel pink (#FFB6D9). The
      outer edge is hot pink (#FF69B4) breaking into angular shards/fragments.
      Small void/shadow particles (#2A1028) begin appearing at the edges,
      mixed with the pink — this is shadow energy, not pure fire.

  F2: FULL SIZE — maximum expansion, approximately 40-48 pixels. A burst of
      pink and dark void energy. The center is pastel pink. The outer ring
      has alternating pink (#FF69B4) and shadow-dark (#2A1028) fragments,
      creating a distinctive "void shatter" look. Angular, crystalline
      break pattern (not round/smooth like fire — sharp like broken glass).

  F3: FADING — the burst collapses inward. Fragments scatter outward and
      become semi-transparent. The center darkens. Void-black (#2A1028)
      wisps curl inward. Pink remnants fading.

  F4: DISSIPATE — nearly gone. A few faint pink pixels and dark shadow
      wisps at the center. Very transparent. Vanishing into nothing.

COLOR PROGRESSION:
  F0: White (#FFFFFF), bright pink (#FFB6D9)
  F1: Pastel pink (#FFB6D9), hot pink (#FF69B4)
  F2: Hot pink (#FF69B4), void black (#2A1028) — the mixed look
  F3: Dark magenta (#8B1A6E), fading pink, shadow wisps
  F4: Faint traces, mostly transparent

DESIGN NOTES:
  - This is a SHADOW/VOID impact, not a fire explosion. The visual should
    feel like reality cracking/shattering with pink energy, not a round
    fiery burst.
  - Angular, crystalline fragments > smooth, round particles.
  - The dark void black mixed with bright pink creates the signature look.

  F1: FLICKER — the afterimage begins distorting. The silhouette splits

      dissolve into upward-floating pink and dark particles/fragments. The

      shape is losing coherence — you can barely tell it was a person.

  F3: VANISH — nearly gone. A few scattered pink (#FF69B4) and dark
      (#2A1028) pixels/fragments floating where the afterimage was.
      Very sparse. Almost fully transparent. Then gone.

  - The "glitch/split" effect in F1 is key to the shadow-tech aesthetic.
  - It dissolves UPWARD into particles (not outward like an explosion).
  - The afterimage faces RIGHT (same as the character's last facing direction).

EFFECT 5: PHANTOM SLASH (Ultimate visual)                           [SHOULD]
================================================================================

File              : phantom_slash.png
Frame size        : 128 x 64 pixels
Frames            : 4
Total sheet size  : 512 x 64 pixels
Animation         : Plays once per slash during the ultimate (5 times total)
Direction         : Horizontal, facing right (engine flips)
Engine adds       : Screen flash, shake, pink particle burst

Layout:
  [     F0     ][     F1     ][     F2     ][     F3     ]
     128x64       128x64       128x64       128x64

FRAME DESCRIPTIONS:
  F0: FLASH — the frame is mostly empty except for a bright horizontal
      line/streak cutting across the center. The streak is approximately
      100 pixels long x 4 pixels tall. Pure white (#FFFFFF) center with
      hot pink (#FF69B4) edges. This represents the instant a phantom slash
      cuts through the space. The streak starts thin on the left and widens
      toward the center, then thins again on the right (speed-line shape).
      Two or three smaller parallel streaks (offset vertically by 8-12px)
      create a multi-slash feel.

  F1: EXPAND — the main streak widens to 6-8 pixels. The color shifts from
      white to bright pastel pink (#FFB6D9). The secondary streaks also
      widen. Dark void cracks (#2A1028) appear between the streaks — like
      reality splitting open where Rina slashed. Angular, sharp.

  F2: SHATTER — the streaks break apart into angular fragments. Pink and
      void-dark shards fly outward from the slash lines. The center of each
      streak darkens to void black. The overall pattern looks like a cracked
      screen/mirror with pink energy leaking through the cracks.

  F3: FADE — the fragments scatter and fade rapidly. The void cracks close.
      A few lingering pink and dark pixels. Nearly transparent. Space heals.

DESIGN NOTES:
  - This is the ULTIMATE effect — most dramatic, largest sprites.
  - The "reality cracking" aesthetic distinguishes this from simple slashes.
  - Multiple parallel streaks make it feel like several rapid cuts at once.
  - The combination of bright pink + void dark is the character's signature.
  - Each play of this animation represents one of the 5 slashes in Phantom
    Barrage. The engine plays it 5 times at different positions.
